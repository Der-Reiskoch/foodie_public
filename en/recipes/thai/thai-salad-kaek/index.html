Thai Salad Kaek

Colorful salad with peanut dressing

Jens (Der Reiskoch) 2025-05-09 Recipes DE/EN
Salad Thai Chicken Shrimp

I recently came across the exciting southern Thai “Salad Kaek” ( สลัดแขก ). This has its origins in Indian or Indonesian cuisine and the name can also be translated as “Indian salad”.

It typically contains a mixture of fried or deep-fried ingredients as well as a selection of fresh vegetables and crispy potato sticks or chips. It is served with a peanut dressing and poured over the salad just before eating.

The selection of ingredients that I present to you here is an example, and you can add to it as you wish or replace individual ingredients with others . The important thing is to create a colorful salad plate that whets the appetite for this unusual salad with its appearance alone.

Ingredients for 2 servings

For the dressing

  • 40g roasted peanuts
  • 2 ½ tbsp red curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1-2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar or brown sugar

For the salad

  • 100g chicken breast
  • 100g shrimp
  • 2 eggs
  • green salad
  • 2 tomatoes
  • half a cucumber
  • a handful of soybean sprouts or other sprouts
  • a handful of Casava- or Potato chips

Preparation

Dressing

  1. crush the peanuts in a mortar until a coarse paste is formed
  2. heat the solid part of the coconut milk in a pan until it thickens
  3. add the red curry paste and stir-fry until the oil separates
  4. add the rest of the coconut milk
  5. add the tamarind paste, fish sauce and sesame oil
  6. add the palm sugar and stir until it is completely dissolved
  7. simmer everything together for about 5 minutes
  8. stir in the crushed peanuts
  9. simmer for a further 2 minutes
  10. pour the dressing into a small bowl and leave to cool

Salad

  1. boil the eggs for 8-10 minutes until they are hard
  2. cut the meat into bite-sized pieces and fry
  3. fry the prawns
  4. cut the green salad into strips
  5. slice the tomatoes and cucumber
  6. peel and quarter the eggs
  7. place the green salad in the center of a large plate
  8. arrange the other ingredients around it
  9. serve the salad with the dressing and potato chips
