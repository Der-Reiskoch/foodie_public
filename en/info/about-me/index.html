Who is “Der Reiskoch” and what does that mean?

Jens (Der Reiskoch) 2021-01-30 DE/EN

Who is “Der Reiskoch”?

“Der Reiskoch” or - translated into english - “The Rice Cook” that is me, Jens Ihrig. I was born in the late 1970s and have lived in Karlsruhe since 2003 before moving to Freiburg in August 2021. I discovered my love for cooking at a young age and the love for Asian cuisines during a three-month trip through Southeast Asia after college in 2002.

Since that first visit to Asia I have been to those beautiful corners of the world more than ten times, each time falling deeper in love with Thailand, its neighbors and their cuisines.

But there will always be a place in my kitchen and on my blog for delicious recipes from all around the world.
