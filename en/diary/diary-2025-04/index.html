Fish and Salad (04-29)

After hiking the whole day, tonight I prepared Thai “Plaa Nueng Si Ju” ( ปลานึ่งซีอิ๊ว ) in the steamer. We also had “Laap Wun Sen” ( ลาบวุ้นเส้น ). We were able to enjoy both in our courtyard in beautiful weather.

Two Thai Dishes (04-19)

For dinner tonight, I prepared a “Yam Moo Thakrai” ( ยำหมูตะไคร้ ) with pork tenderloin, as well as a steamed “Mok Gai” ( หมกไก่ ) from Isaan.

Khao Niau Piak Lam Yai (04-17)

Tonight, I served some of our very ripe Longan fruit with sticky rice and coconut milk as the Thai dessert “Khao Niau Piak Lam Yai” ( ข้าวเหนียวเปียกลำไย ). For the first time, I colored the sticky rice blue using butterfly pea flowers

Salad and Eggs (04-16)

For dinner tonight, I made steamed eggs with minced pork, or “Khai Toon Moo Sap” ( ไข่ตุ๋นหมูสับ ). I also had a fake “Yam Hua Plee” ( ยำหัวปลี ) on the side, using chicory instead of banana blossom.

Recommended Dampfgarer Set at amazon.de¹

Khao Pad Pong Gari Gai (04-14)

During my lunch break, I used leftover rice to make Thai “Khao Pad Pong Gari Gai” ( ข้าวผัดผงกะหรี่ไก่ ). For this fried rice, I used chicken, chopped vegetables, and yellow curry powder.

Gaeng Som with omelet (04-11)

For lunch today, I made a “Gaeng Som Plaa” ( แกงส้มปลา ) with cod and luffa squash. As a side dish, I had an omelet with wild garlic. I prepared this spicy curry from Southern Thailand using Gaeng Som curry paste from Yak Thai.

Yam Khai Dao (04-09)

For dinner tonight, I had a Thai “Yam Khai Dao” ( ยำไข่ดาว ). That’s a delicious salad with fried eggs and fresh herbs.

Nam Prik (04-08)

Tonight I made myself a Northern Thai “Nam Prik Makuea Thet” ( น้ำพริกมะเขือเทศ ) with tomatoes, chilies, and quite a lot of herbs. For dipping, I had pork cracklings and cucumber slices, and to wash it all down, an ice-cold Leo.

Gai Pad Nor Mai Farang (04-07)

Looking forward to asparagus season, I whipped up a quick Thai stir-fry for lunch: Gai Pad Nor Mai Farang ( ไก่ผัดหน่อไม้ฝรั่ง ) with chicken and green asparagus.

Salad & Gai Thod (04-04)

For dinner tonight, I made a “Yam Wun Sen” ( ยำวุ้นเส้น ). I also had chicken drumsticks on the side as Gai Thod Nam Plaa ( ไก่ทอดน้ำปลา ) from the air fryer.

Recommended Bella Airfryer at amazon.de¹

Yam Khai Khon (04-02)

Tonight I made a simple Thai Yam Khai Khon ( ยำไข่คน ) with scrambled eggs, shallots, and cilantro. This salad is easily done, but very delicious.

Pad Thai (04-01)

For lunch today, I made Pad Thai ( ผัดไทย ) with chicken and shrimp. I tried the Pad Thai sauce from Yak Thai for this dish. I was very happy with the result, but I added a little tamarind paste because it was lacking some acidity
