Hed Dong
Jens2025-06-30

Yesterday I pickled shimeji mushrooms and enoki mushrooms in fish sauce and salt, then cooked them tonight and mixed them with chillies, shallots and lime juice. I got the recipe for these Hed Dong ( เห็ดดอง ) from Alex at Yak Thai.

Yam Mama
Jens2025-06-29

After a long hike, I prepared a quick and easy Thai yam mama ( ยำมาม่า ) as a very late lunch today.

Suea Rong Hai
Jens2025-06-28

Tonight the tiger had to cry again, because I prepared a rump steak as a Thai Suea Rong Hai ( สือรองไฮ ) with a very spicy dip.

Gaeng Som Cha Om Khai
Jens2025-06-25

Today I prepared a Thai “Gaeng Som Cha Om Khai” ( แกงส้มชะอมไข่ ) for lunch. I used the Gaeng Som Paste from Yak Thai for this.

Yam Hed
Jens2025-06-24

For dinner today I prepared a simple Thai Yam Hed ( ยำเห็ด ) with fried oyster mushrooms, chilis and coriander.

Pad Woon Sen Cha Om Goong
Jens2025-06-24

Today for lunch I made a Thai Pad Woon Sen Cha Om Goong ( ผัดวุ้นเส้นชะอมกุ้ง ) with glass noodles, acacia shoots, prawns and egg.

Pad Phed Sator Goong
Jens2025-06-17

I prepared a Thai Pad Phed Sator Goong for lunch today with the sator beans from the Asian grocery store. I used the southern Thai curry paste from Yak Thai, which gave the dish quite a bit of heat.

Gai Pad Met Ma Muang
Jens2025-06-13

After a short hike in the morning, I made myself a quick Thai Gai Pad Met Ma Muang with chicken, bok choy, baby corn and cashews for lunch today.

Goong Pad Makham
Jens2025-06-10

During the lunch break today we had fried prawns again, this time in the form of a Thai Goong Pad Makham ( กุ้งผัดมะขาม ) with tamarind paste, fish sauce and a few chilies.

Pad Ga Prau Goong
Jens2025-06-09

In the evening, I prepared some super spicy Pad Ga Prau Goong ( ผัดกะเพรากุ้ง ). Of course, served with a fried egg.

Goong Pad Nam Prik Pao
Jens2025-06-08

For lunch today, I fried prawns together with nam prik pao and Thai basil as [Goong Pad Nam Prik Pao](/ahaan-thai/en/index/g/goong- pad-nam-prik-pao/) ( กุ้งผัดน้ำพริกเผา ). I used the “Nam Prik Pao Extra Hot” from Yak Thai for this.

Fish and Corn
Jens2025-06-07

Tonight I prepared steamed fish with a spicy tomato dip from Isaan, accompanied by grilled corn on the cob with a salty-sweet coconut sauce. The recipe for the Plaa Nueng Jaew Makuea Thet ( ปลานึ่งแจ่วมะเขือเทศ ) comes from “Thailand: The Cookbook” and the one for the Khao Phot Ping from “Pok Pok” by Andy Ricker.

For lunch today, I prepared a Burmese “Lahpet Thok” and a “Kebat” with chicken. Both recipes come from the book “Burma Superstar”.

This evening I prepared a green Thai curry with chicken and purple mini eggplants. As the basis for this Gaeng Keow Wan Gai ( แกงเขียวหวานไก่ ) I used a curry paste from Yak Thai.

Laap Khua Hed Moo Sap
Jens2025-06-03

For dinner, I prepared a northern Thai “Laap Khua Hed Moo Sap” ( ลาบคั่วเห็ดหมูสับ ) with mushrooms and minced pork. I served this salad with chicken drumsticks and corn from the air fryer.

Choo Chee Plaa
Jens2025-06-03

Today I prepared a southern Thai Choo Chee Plaa ( ฉู่ฉี่ปลา ) with cod fish and a somewhat too thin sauce. The sauce was prepared with the red curry paste from Yak Thai, so it was a bit too thin, but very tasty.

Red Curry Paste

Pad Rak Bua Moo Sap
Jens2025-06-02

During my lunch break today, I prepared a quick Thai Pad Rak Bua Moo Sap ( ผัดรากบัวหมูสับ ) with lotus root and ground pork.

Gaeng Taepo
Jens2025-06-01

This evening, I prepared a Thai Gaeng Taepo ( แกงเทโพ ) with pork, oyster mushrooms, and pea eggplants. The idea for the mushrooms came from my good, old yellow curry cookbook.
