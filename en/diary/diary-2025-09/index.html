Laap Woon Sen
Jens2025-09-29

Tonight i prepared a spicy Thai Laap Woon Sen ( ลาบวุ้นเส้น ) with minced pork and glass noodles for dinner again.

Northern Thai Again
Jens2025-09-28

For dinner tonight there were three dishes from Northern Thailand again. The first was Ob Gai Asii Pian ( อบไก่อสีเปียน ), a Shan curry with chicken and many herbs. The next dish was Gai Thod Makhwaen ( ไก่ทอดมะแขว่น ) or chicken drumsticks with Makhwaen. Additionally, there was Pak Nam Saa ( ผักน้ำส้า ) with pennywort on the table again. Both new dishes also come from Austin Bush’s cookbook The Food of Northern Thailand.

Recommended The Food of Northern Thailand at amazon.de¹

Mae Hong Son Menu
Jens2025-09-27

Tonight there were three dishes from the northern Thai province Mae Hong Son. First a Gaeng Hung Lay Tai ( แกงฮั่งเล่ไต ) with pork belly, then a noodle soup with minced meat, canned fish and tomatoes called Khao Sen ( ข้าวเส้น ) and also a Pak Nam Saa ( ผักน้ำส้า ) with Indian pennywort. All three dishes are from the book “The Food of Northern Thailand” by Austin Bush.

Recommended The Food of Northern Thailand at amazon.de¹

Two Mok Dishes
Jens2025-09-26

For dinner there were two steamed dishes from Isaan. First Mok Hed ( หมกเห็ด ) with various mushrooms and then Mok Normai ( หมกหน่อไม้ ) with bamboo shoots, pork belly, coconut milk and various spices according to a recipe from the book “Hawker Fare” by James Syhabout. Both dishes are normally wrapped in banana leaves and steamed in them, but unfortunately I couldn’t get any.

Recommended Hawker Fare at amazon.de¹

Hed Hom Thod See Ew
Jens2025-09-26

Today for lunch I prepared Thai Hed Hom Thod See Ew ( เห็ดหอมทอดซีอิ๊ว ) in the wok. These shiitake mushrooms in soy sauce according to a recipe from the book “Classic Thai Stir Fry Dishes” by Hanuman Aspler are a real umami bomb!

Recommended Classic Thai Stir Fry Dishes at amazon.de¹

Si Khrong Moo Thod
Jens2025-09-22

For dinner I prepared Thai Si Khrong Moo Thod Gratiam Prik Thai ( ซี่โครงหมูทอดกระเทียมพริกไทย ) in the air fryer. These crispy ribs are seasoned with a marinade of various sauces plus garlic and crushed pepper.

Jok Moo Sap

Since there was no bread left in the house this morning and it was pouring rain, I had Jok Moo Sap ( โจ๊กหมูสับ ) for a Thai breakfast with left over rice from the weekend, broth, and minced pork.

Khanom Bang Rachini
Jens2025-09-18

Today for lunch I implemented an idea that had been floating around in my head for a long time. I made vol-au-vents filled with Thai Rad Na ( ราดหน้า ) and I quite boldly call them “Khanom Bang Rachini Rad Na Moo” ( ขนมปังราชินีราดหน้าหมู ). They tasted very good, just as I had imagined.
