Sweet and Sour
Jens2024-05-31

What I really can’t stand in Asian restaurants is everything in sweet and sour sauce. That’s why I’ve never tried the Thai version of it before. Today I dared and made a “Moo Pad Priau Wan” (หมูผัดเปรี้ยวหวาน). I prepared that according to the recipe from the book “Sabai” from Hot Thai Kitchen and apart from the cucumbers I found it surprisingly tasty. In addition there was also a sweet-sour-spicy “Tam Strawberry” (ตำสตรอเบอร์รี่) with fresh strawberries from the Münstermarkt.

Recommended Sabai at amazon.de¹

Kanaa Moo Grob
Jens2024-05-31

This lunchtime I prepared a quick Thai “Pad Pak Kanaa Moo Grob” (ผัดผักคะน้าหมูกรอบ ) with the remaining crispy pork belly and fresh Chinese broccoli, which I was lucky enough to get in the Asian supermarket.

Salad and Curry
Jens2024-05-30

Tonight, I prepared a “Gaeng Panaeng” (แกงพะแนง) with chicken based on a very spicy curry paste, that I had brought with me from Thailand and a “Yam Moo Grob” (ยำหมูกรอบ ) with crispy pork belly.

Two Thai Salads
Jens2024-05-29

This evening we had two Thai salads for dinner. First there was a southern Thai “Yam Bai Chaploo” (ยำใบชะพลู) with wild betel pepper leafs and chicken and then a “Yam Moo Sam Chan” (ยำหมูสามชั้น) with fried pork belly.

Sen Yai Two Ways
Jens2024-05-26

Today I made another large portion of Thai wide rice noodles or “Sen Yai” (เส้นใหญ่) from rice paper. At lunchtime is used same for “Sen Yai Rad Na” (เส้นใหญ่ราดหน้า) and in the evening we had “Sen Yai Yentafo” (เส้นใหญ่เย็นตาโฟ).

Gaeng Panaeng Nuea
Jens2024-05-25

Tonight I made a Thai “Gaeng Panaeng” (แกงพะแนง) with beef using a recipe from SeriousEats.com. What was special about it was that grilled shallots were used as a vegetable garnish, which gave the curry a slightly smoky note.

Southern Thai Meal
Jens2024-05-24

My copy of the book “The Food of Southern Thailand” by Austin Bush finally arrived. With the help of this I prepared a “Gaeng Khua Poo Bai Chaploo” (แกงคั่วปูใบชะพลู) with crab meat and wild betel pepper leaves as well as the southern Thai rice salad “Khao Yam Phak Dai” (ข้าวยำป ักษ์ใต้ ) for dinner.

Recommended The Food of Southern Thailand at amazon.de¹

Two Thai dishes
Jens2024-05-24

This lunch I prepared a “Sai Bua Pad Goong” (สายบัวผัดกุ้ง) with lotus stems and shrimps. As a second dish there was “Gai Thod Nam Prik Pao” (ไก่ทอดน้ำพริกเผา) based on a recipe from my new cookbook “Hawker Fair” by James Syhabout.

Recommended Hawker Fare at amazon.de¹

Dok Gui Chai Pad Moo
Jens2024-05-23

Today we had a quick Thai “Dok Gui Chai Pad Mhu” (ดอกกุยช่ายผัดหมู) with flowering chives and pork for lunch.

Two Thai Salads
Jens2024-05-22

This evening we had two Thai salads for dinner. First there was a “Yam Mara Goong” (ยำมะระกุ้ง) with bitter gourd and shrimp and then a “Yam Saam Sahai” (ยำสามสหาย) with two types of meat and more shrimp.

Tom Saep Kra Dhuk Moo
Jens2024-05-22

Today we had a “Tom Saep Kra Dhuk Moo” (ต้มแซ่บกระดูกหมู) from Isaan for lunch. This sour and spicy soup with pork ribs had been simmering all morning. For preparing it, I followed a recipe from the book “Hot Thai Kitchen”.

Recommended Hot Thai Kitchen at amazon.de¹

Khao Niew Ma Muang
Jens2024-05-21

Although it has been one of Anne’s favorite dishes for a long time, I prepared “Khao Niew Ma Muang” (ข้าวเหนียวมะม่วง), or better known as “Mango Sticky Rice” this evening for the very first time by myself.

Pad See Ew Gai
Jens2024-05-21

Even though the Chinese broccoli leftovers from the weekend were a little wilted and looked sad, I used them to prepare a Thai “Pad See Ew” (ผัดซีอิ้ว) with chicken for lunch. Today I made the wide “Sen Yai” rice noodles from rectangular rice paper.

Three Thai Dishes
Jens2024-05-17

Today I prepared some of the “Moo Dad Deaw” (หมูแดดเดียว) that I planned for the weekend. As dipping sachace there was “Nam Djim Djau” (น้ำจิ้มแจ่ว). I also made a “Yam Moo Yang” (ยำหมูย่าง ) and a “Yam Saam Sahai” (ยำสามสหาย).

Gai Khem and Soup
Jens2024-05-16

Today I prepared a Thai soup named “Tom Mara Gra Duk Moo” (ต้มมะระกระดูกหมู ) with bitter gourd and pork ribs. As a taste counterpart, there was also a sweet and salty “Gai Khem” (ไก่เค็ม) based on a recipe from the book “Sabai” by Hot Thai Kitchen.

Recommended Sabai at amazon.de¹

Mara Pad Khai
Jens2024-05-15

Today’s lunch was a quick Thai “Mara Pad Khai” (มะระผัดไข่ ) with bitter melon and scrumbled eggs.

Two Thai Dishes
Jens2024-05-08

Tonight we had a Thai “Yam Gai” (ยำไก่) with fried turkey breast. I also tried a very harmless version of a Northern Thai “Saa Nuea” (ส้าเนื้อ).

Pad Ka Prao Moo Sap
Jens2024-05-08

Today, I prepared once again a Thai Pad Ka Prao Moo Sap (ผัดกะเพราหมูสับ) with rice and two fried eggs.

Khao Pad Sapparot
Jens2024-05-07

When I came into the kitchen this morning, it smelled of the ripe pineapple that I bought last week. I took this as an opportunity to prepare a Thai “Khao Pad Sapparot” (ข้าวผัดสับปะรด) with seafood during my lunch break. I based it on a recipe from the book “Hot Thai Kitchen”.

Recommended Hot Thai Kitchen at amazon.de¹

Tiger & Amaranth
Jens2024-05-06

With the great steaks that Anne had bought at the Münstermarkt, I prepared “Thai Suea Rong Hai” (สุกี้แห้ง) or “Crying Tiger” based on a recipe by Leela Punyaratabandhu on seriouseats.com. This was accompanied by fried amaranth in two different ways. First i did a “Pad Pak Khom Nam Man Hoi” (ผัดผักโขมน้ำมันหอย) with oyster sauce and then a “Pad Pak Khom Tao Hu” (ผัดผักโขมเต้าหู้) with tofu and garlic.
