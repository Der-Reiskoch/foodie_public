My culinary adventures

Guai Tiao Nuea Sap (12-15)

For lunch today, I prepared wide rice noodles with a Northernthai sauce made from minced meat, shallots, and tomatoes. The recipe for the dish is from Andy Ricker’s book “Pok Pok Noodles” and is called “Guai Tiao Nuea Sap” (ก๋วยเตี๋ยวเนื้อสับ) in the book.

Recommended Pok Pok Noodles at amazon.de*

Dry Curry (12-11)

Tonight I made a dry Thai chicken curry with a paste made from chilies, lemongrass, turmeric, and salt. The dish is called “Gai Khua Sa Moon Prai” (ไก่คั่วสมุนไพ) and it’s absolutely delicious.

Laap Woon Sen (12-10)

Tonight’s dinner was “Laap Woon Sen” (ลาบวุ้นเส้น), a delicious fusion of the spicy northeastern Thai minced meat salad and a refreshing glass noodle salad. I can’t believe I never thought to combine these flavors before!

Laap and Curry (12-06)

Tonight, I made a special dinner for my friends from Switzerland: “Laap Moo” and a dry green curry with fish balls and eggplant. The curry, which is called “Gaeng Kiaw Wan Luk Chin Plaa Pad Haeng” (แกงเขียวหวานลูกชิ้นปลาผัดแห้ง) in Thai, is a recipe I found in the cookbook “Sabai” by Hot Thai Kitchen.

Recommended Sabai at amazon.de*

Green Curry with Noodles (12-03)

I enjoyed a delicious bowl of green Thai curry with beef and rice noodles for lunch. This dish is known as “Khanom Chin Gaeng Khiao Wan Nuea” (ขนมจีนแกงเขียวหวานเนื้อ) in Thai.
