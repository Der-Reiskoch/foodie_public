Laap Moo Khua
Jens2025-12-10

Over the weekend I had prepared a larger batch of “Prik Laap”. With this intense spice blend from Northern Thailand, I made a Laap Moo Khua ( ลาบหมูคั่ว ) for lunch today.

Sevgin Ocakbaşi
Jens2025-12-09

During a walk through the Stühlinger district, Anne and I finally stopped at “Sevgin Ocakbaşi”. This Turkish grill house had been recommended to us several times. We ordered the Adana Kebab, hummus and a farmer’s salad. The skewers, bread and side dishes were very delicious, only the farmer’s salad was unusually sweet.

Khai Jiew Dok Seno
Jens2025-12-09

With the leftover Yellow Sesbania flowers I made a Thai Khai Jiew Dok Seno ( ไข่เจียวดอกโสน ) for lunch today. In addition to the flowers, I also used spring onions and shallots in this omelet.

Two Thai Salads
Jens2025-12-08

For dinner tonight, I had two Thai salads. First, a Yum Dok Seno ( ยำดอกโสน ) with yellow Sesbania, chicken, and shrimp, as well as a Laap Goong ( ลาบกุ้ง ) with coarsely chopped shrimp.

Pad Pak Kwang Tung Moo Grob
Jens2025-12-08

While shopping at the Asian grocery store, I once again grabbed the wrong thing and bought the second cousin Choi Sam instead of Chinese broccoli. But this type of mustard greens can also be prepared in the style of Thai Pad Pak Kanaa Moo Grob together with crispy pork belly. The whole thing could be called “Pad Pak Kwang Tung Moo Grob” ( ผัดผักกวางตุ้งหมูกรอบ ).

Big Pork Feast
Jens2025-12-06

This evening we had a spontaneous visit from our old friend Sascha. He had announced himself for dinner and said he would bring some things from food sharing. The large bag full of groceries he brought threw my original cooking plans overboard, but instead treated us to delicious Thai pork dishes.

During a fun kitchen party, we prepared Nam Tok Moo ( น้ำตกหมู ), Moo Pad Priau Wan ( หมูผัดเปรี้ยวหวาน ) and Moo Thod Gratiam ( หมูทอดกระเทียม ). As an appetizer, we had some Luk Chin Ping ( ลูกชิ้นปิ้ง ) with the homemade Nam Jim Luk Chin ( น้ำจิ้มลูกชิ้น ) that I had prepared this morning.

Khao Pad Poo
Jens2025-12-06

Before I left for hiking this morning, I had a quick Thai Khao Pad Poo ( ข้าวผัดปู ) with crayfish and egg as a late breakfast or early lunch.

Luk Chin Ping and Salad
Jens2025-12-05

This evening I grilled some of the Luk Chin and served them as Luk Chin Ping ( ลูกชิ้นปิ้ง ). The sweet-sour-spicy Nam Jim Luk Chin ( น้ำจิ้มลูกชิ้น ) dip was simply perfect with the delicious meatballs. I also made a Yam Gai ( ยำไก่ ) with chicken, baby corn, cherry tomatoes and fresh herbs.

Luk Chin and Nam Jim
Jens2025-12-04

This afternoon I picked up a package with the latest products from Yak Thai at the parcel shop. Among other things, it contained one package each of Luk Chin ( ลูกชิ้น ) made from beef and pork, as well as the matching sauce Nam Jim Luk Chin ( น้ำจิ้มลูกชิ้น ), which is not yet available in the shop.

Gai Pad Nga Khao
Jens2025-12-04

During my lunch break today, I prepared a quick Thai Gai Pad Nga Khao ( ไก่ผัดงาขาว ) with chicken and sesame seeds. The recipe came from the book “Cuisines of the World: Thailand”.

Yam Moo Yor
Jens2025-12-03

This evening I made another faked Thai Yam Moo Yor ( ยำหมูยอ ) with bologna sausage, baby corn, cherry tomatoes, chilies and fresh herbs.

Pad See Ew Ba Mee
Jens2025-12-01

During my lunch break today, I prepared a quick Thai Pad See Ew Ba Mee ( ผัดซีอิ๊วบะหมี่ ) with Chinese egg noodles, pork and broccolini.
