Khai Soi Gai
Jens2025-07-31

For lunch today, I prepared a quick northern Thai Khao Soi Gai ( ข้าวซอยไก่ ) with chicken breast and instant noodles. I used the Khao Soi Paste from Yak Thai for this.

Salads and Watermelon
Jens2025-07-30

I prepared a Yum Moo Yor ( ยำหมูยอ ) from the chili moo yor from Yak Thai in the evening. We also had Thaeng Mo Plaa Haeng ( แตงโมปลาแห้ง ) with watermelon and dried fish. I then used the watermelon rind to make a Som Tam Plueak Thaeng Mo ( ส้มตำเปลือกแตงโม ).

Two Thai dishes
Jens2025-07-28

Today at lunchtime, I made Gaeng Phed Moo Sap ( แกงเผ็ดหมูสับ ) for Anne using the leftovers from the red Thai curry with duck breast.. For myself, I then made a Pad Hed Hom Nam Man Hoi ( ผัดเห็ดหอมน้ำมันหอย ) with fresh shiitake mushrooms. The recipe for the mushrooms came from the book “Thailand: The Cookbook”.

Recommended Thailand: The Cookbook at amazon.de¹

Gaeng Phed Bpet
Jens2025-07-27

It’s been a while, but tonight I cooked a Thai Gaeng Phed Bpet ( แกงเผ็ดเป็ด ) with grilled duck breast, Thai eggplants and cocktail tomatoes.

Miang Plaa
Jens2025-07-26

My two Bai Chaplu plants have grown a lot of new leaves in the last few weeks. So I came up with the idea of using some of them for Thai Miang Plaa ( เมี่ยงปลา ).

Salad and Hor Mok
Jens2025-07-25

For dinner today, I prepared Thai Hor Mok Khanom Krok ( ห่อหมกขนมครก ). I prepared these steamed snacks with fish and coconut milk in my takoyaki pan by I prepared these steamed snacks with fish and coconut milk in my takoyaki pan by placing them in my steamer with the handle removed. To accompany this delicious snack, which we discovered in Ranong, I also made Yum Makuea Yao Moo Sap ( ยำมะเขือยาวหมูสับ ) with Japanese eggplant and minced meat.

Takoyaki pan at amazon.de¹
Steam cooker set at amazon.de¹

Pad Ga Prau Moo Grob
Jens2025-07-23

My former colleague Stephan got me hooked with a post on Facebook, so today i cooked a Thai Pad Ga Prau Moo Grob ( ผัดกะเพราหมูกรอบ ) with crispy pork belly for lunch. To make it, I prepared Pork Belly in the Airfryer in the morning.

Recommended Bella Airfryer at amazon.de¹

Yum Hed
Jens2025-07-22

This evening, I prepared a Thai Yum Hed ( ยำเห็ด ) with a selection of mushrooms from the “Münstermarkt” (Daily market next to the cathedral) in Freiburg.

Gaeng Massaman Moo
Jens2025-07-22

Today for lunch I cooked a Thai Gaeng Massaman Moo ( แกงมัสมั่นหมู ) with pork and potatoes. The idea to use pork in a Massaman Curry is from the book “Taste of Thailand” by Jan Wischnewski.

Recommended Taste of Thailand at amazon.de¹

Pad Kee Mao
Jens2025-07-20

Today for lunch i cooked a super spicy Thai Pad Kee Mao ( ผัดขี้เมา ) with pork and thin rice noodles.

Soup and Curry
Jens2025-07-19

For dinner today I cooked a Thai Tom Saep Nuea ( ต้มแซ่บเนื้อ ) with beef, mushrooms and tomatoes and a Gaeng Paa Moo ( แกงป่าหมู ) with pork and fresh vegetables .

Yum Nuea
Jens2025-07-18

Tonight I made a quick Thai Yum Nuea ( ยำน้ำยา ) with beef and fresh herbs. It’s so simple, but super tasty!

Pad Ga Prau Moo Yor
Jens2025-07-18

My day off today started with a Thai Pad Ga Prau Moo Yor ( ผัดกะเพราหมูยอ ). It seems to be a good base, so I went for a 35km hike.

Laap Hed Woon Sen
Jens2025-07-16

For Dinner today, I had another Thai salad with glass noodles. This time it was a Laap Hed Woon Sen ( ลาบเห็ดวุ้นเส้น ) with chopped mushrooms, some minced meat and fresh herbs.

Yum Ruam Mit
Jens2025-07-15

Tonight I prepared a Thai Yum Ruam Mit ( ยำรวมมิตร ) with glass noodles, minced meat, prawns, squid rings, sliced sausage and cocktail tomatoes according to a recipe from the book “Cooking with Poo” by Saiyuud Diwong.

Recommended Cooking with Poo at amazon.de¹

Pad Ga Prau Nuea
Jens2025-07-15

Today for lunch I made a spicy Thai Pad Ga Prau Nuea ( ผัดกะเพราเนื้อ ) with beef and holy basil from my herb garden. It was served with jasmine rice and, of course, a fried egg.

Two Thai Noodle Dishes
Jens2025-07-13

Two Thai noodle dishes from leftovers: for Anne I prepared a Pad Thai ( ผัดไทย ) with chicken and for me a Pad Mama Hor Khai ( ผัดมาม่าห่อไข่ ), these are fried instant noodles served in an omelette.

Salad and Curry
Jens2025-07-12

For dinner today, I prepared a Yum Hed Dong ( ยำเห็ดดอง ) with fermented mushrooms . As a contrast to this, there was also a “Gaeng Panaeng Nuea” ( แกงพะแนงเนื้อ ).

Pad Thai Moo
Jens2025-07-12

Today for lunch I prepared a quick Thai Pad Thai ( ผัดไทย ) with pork. I used the excellent Pad Thai sauce from Yak Thai for this.

Yam Goong Sod
Jens2025-07-09

In the evening, I prepared a rather spicy Thai Yam Goong Sod ( ยำกุ้งสด ) with prawns and cocktail tomatoes.

Tom Kha Gai
Jens2025-07-08

For dinner, I made a Thai Tom Kha Gai ( ต้มข่าไก่ ) for the first time in a long time. Out of curiosity, I also used coconut milk powder in addition to the coconut milk, which made the soup very creamy.

Yam Moo Yor
Jens2025-07-07

For dinner today, I prepared a Thai Yam Moo Yor ( ยำหมูยอร์ ). I used poultry sausage instead of Moo Yor for this salad.

Gai Pad Khing
Jens2025-07-07

Back in Freiburg, I prepared a Thai Gai Pad Khing ( ไก่ผัดขิง ) for lunch today. I used mini corncobs as a rather unusual vegetable for this dish.

Gai Pad Kha
Jens2025-07-01

Today for lunch I prepared a southern Thai Gai Pad Kha ( ไก่ผัดไข่ ) with chicken, fresh galangal and lots of chilis.
