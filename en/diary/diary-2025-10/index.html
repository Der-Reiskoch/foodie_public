Yum Makuea and Sai Ua
Jens2025-10-10

This evening I pan-fried Northern Thai Sai Ua ( ไส้อั่ว ). As a side dish, there was a Yum Makuea Matua ( ยำมะเขือมะตัว ) with eggplants, snake beans, bell peppers and minced meat. The recipe for the salad comes from the book “The Food of Northern Thailand” by Austin Bush.

Hed Nueng Nam Prik Kha
Jens2025-10-05

This evening I tried another recipe from Austin Bush’s book The Food of Northern Thailand. It was steamed mushrooms with a spicy galangal chili dip, or in Thai Hed Nueng Nam Prik Kha ( เห็ดนึ่งน้ำพริกข่า ).

Tom Dok Mai Jin Hed Hom
Jens2025-10-04

For dinner I prepared a Chinese-Thai Tom Dok Mai Jin Hed Hom ( ต้มดอกไม้จีนเห็ดหอม ) with pork spare ribs, dried lily flowers and shiitake mushrooms.

Khao Mok Gai
Jens2025-10-04

For lunch today I prepared a type of Khao Mok Gai ( ข้าวหมกไก่ ) with leftover rice. It was served with chicken drumsticks from the air fryer.

Yum Kra Jiab Moo Sap Goong
Jens2025-10-03

For lunch today I prepared another Thai Yum Kra Jiab ( ยำกระเจี๊ยบ ). This time a bit more elaborate with carrots, shrimp and minced meat.

Salad and Stir-Fry
Jens2025-10-02

Tonight I prepared Khua Khae Gai ( คั่วแคะไก่ ) from Northern Thailand. As a side dish, there was also a simple salad with okra or in Thai Yum Kra Jiab ( ยำกระเจี๊ยบ ).

Yum Sapparot Phuket
Jens2025-10-01

For dinner tonight there was a Southern Thai salad with pineapple and shrimp. The recipe for this Yum Sapparot Phuket ( ยำสับปะรดภูเก็ต ) comes from the book “Thailand: The Cookbook” by Jean-Pierre Gabriel.
