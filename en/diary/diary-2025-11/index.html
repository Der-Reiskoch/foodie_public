Thai Salad and Soup
Jens2025-11-29

This evening I prepared a Thai Yum Som O ( ยำส้มโอ ) with pomelo, prawns and hearts of palm. We also had a northern Thai Khao Soi Moo ( ข้าวซอยหมู ), which I prepared using the Khao Soi paste from Yak Thai.

Pad Cha Gai
Jens2025-11-26

During my lunch break today, I prepared a spicy Thai Pad Cha Gai ( ผัดฉ่าไก่ ) with chicken and various vegetables.

Rad Na Gai
Jens2025-11-25

At lunch today, I couldn’t quite think of what to cook. But Thai Rad Na ( ราดหน้า ) always works somehow. This time with chicken and Bimi.

Yum Thua Phu Moo Sap
Jens2025-11-24

For dinner this evening, I prepared a Thai Yum Thua Phu Moo Sap ( ยำถั่วพูหมูสับ ) with winged beans and minced pork.

Kuai Tiao Nam Ngiao
Jens2025-11-23

Inspired by a post from “Hot Thai Kitchen”, I made a Kuai Tiao Nam Ngiao ( ก๋วยเตี๋ยวน้ำเงี้ยว ) this evening. Although I didn’t have dried cotton tree flowers or blood tofu, I was able to use one of the Thua Nao Khaep discs I had brought back from northern Thailand and added some blood powder.

Three Thai Dishes
Jens2025-11-22

This evening, we had a visit from our old friend Arek from Karlsruhe. Since he’s currently eating without carbs, I prepared three Thai dishes that can be served without rice. There was Nam Prik Makuea Yao ( น้ำพริกมะเขือยาว ) with various side dishes for dipping. Plus a Sa Loem Jin Yang ( สเลิ้มจิ้นย่าง ) as well as pork tenderloin with Nam Jim Jeaw ( น้ำจิ้มแจ่ว ).

Gai Pad Bai Cha Plu
Jens2025-11-21

For lunch today, I made a quick Thai Gai Pad Bai Cha Plu ( ไก่ผัดใบชะพลู ) with chicken and wild betel pepper leaves.

Gaeng Phed Fak Tong
Jens2025-11-20

During my lunch break today, I prepared a vegetarian red Thai curry with pumpkin. For this Gaeng Phed Fak Thong ( แกงเผ็ดฟักทอง ) I should write a recipe when I get the chance.

Busiate alla Tailandese
Jens2025-11-18

Fusion alert! This lunch, my new favorite pasta met my beloved Thai dish. Busiate alla Tailandese with plenty of chilies, minced meat and Yak Thai’s Ga Prau sauce. And of course a fried egg, with fermented tofu instead of Parmesan.

Nam Prik Ong
Jens2025-11-17

For a gentle transition between three weeks of Sicilian cuisine back to Thai cuisine, this evening featured the northern Thai dip Nam Prik Ong ( น้ำพริกอ่อง ).

Busiate al Pesto di Pistaccio
Jens2025-11-17

I discovered my new favorite pasta while in Sicily: busiate. These long, twisted noodles can absorb a surprising amount of sauce or pesto. I brought a package back and served it this afternoon with pistachio pesto.
