Khao Soi Nuea (11-30)

After Anne and I caught a cold, soup was in order. And what better choice than Northern “Thai Khao Soi Nuea” (ข้าวซอยเนื้อ)?

Guai Tiao Nam Moo (11-27)

During the second day of our hackathon, my coworkers and I decided to take a break at “Chang”, a Thai restaurant nearby. For lunch, I ordered a “Guay Tiao Nam Moo” (ก๋วยเตี๋ยวน้ำหมู), which is a delicious pork noodle soup.

Sen Yai Pad Kee Mao (11-15)

I treated myself to a very spicy lunch today with “Sen Yai Pad Kee Mao” (เส้นใหญ่ผัดขี้เมา). I made the extra-wide rice noodles myself using rice paper.

Nam Prik and Yam Nang (11-13)

Tonight, I took another culinary trip to Northern Thailand. I made a “Yam Nang” (ยำหนัง) salad, which is traditionally made with dried water buffalo skin, but I substituted it with beef jerky. I also cheated a bit with the “Nam Prik Aep Thua Nao” (น้ำพริกแอบถั่วเน่า) and used yellow bean paste instead of fresh fermented soybeans.

Two Thai Egg Dishes (11-12)

Tonight, we had two Thai egg dishes. One was “Khai Tom Rad Nam Yam” (ไข่ต้มราดน้ำยำ), which is hard-boiled eggs with a spicy salad dressing. The other was steamed “Khai Toon” (ไข่ตุ๋น) with shrimp and mushrooms.

Khao Pad Fak Thong (11-11)

Leftover jasmine rice and pumpkin got a makeover today! I turned them into a flavorful Thai “Khao Pad Fak Thong” (ข้าวผัดฟักทอง). It’s a simple yet satisfying fried rice dish with pumpkin.

Gai Hor Bai Toey Fake (11-08)

Tonight, I tried to fake Thai “Gai Hor Bai Toey” (ไก่ใบเตย) or “chicken in pandan leaf” by adding pandan extract while frying the chicken. Spoiler: it didn’t really work!

Nam Prik und Moo Lung (11-07)

I treated myself to a delicious Northern Thai meal tonight:“Nam Prik Nor Mai Dong” (น้ำพริกหน่อไม้ดอง) dip and “Moo Lung” (หมูลุง) meatballs.

Gai Pad Khing Ka Prao (11-06)

For lunch today, I made a Thai dish called “Gai Pad Khing Ka Prao” (ไก่ผัดขิงกะเพรา) with chicken, ginger, and holy basil. The recipe is from the german cookbook “Bangkok Original Streetfood” by Ben Kindler.

Recommended Bangkok Original Streetfood at amazon.de¹

Northern Thai cuisine (11-05)

Tonight, I took another culinary trip into Northern Thail cuisine. I made “Nam Prik Nor Mai Don"g (น้ำพริกหน่อไม้ดอง) with pickled bamboo shoots and a “Laap Gai” with Prik Laap. Both recipes are from the exciting Lanna Food Collection of Chiang Mai University.

Pad Prik Tao Hu Nor Mai (11-04)

Today, I prepared a speedy Thai lunch of “Pad Prik Tao Hu Nor Mai” (ผัดพริกเต้าหู้หน่อไม้). That is a stir-fry dish made with tofu, bamboo shoots, and green beans
